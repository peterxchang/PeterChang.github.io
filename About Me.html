About Me

A few interesting things about me: I'm currently a fourth-year Business Honors student who enjoys staying active! Growing up in a suburban neighborhood in Chicago, I spent most of my days outside playing various sports, including football, soccer, ultimate frisbee, and wiffle ball (the "anti-window-breaking" form of baseball). Today, I still carry most of these interests with me through joining an intramural soccer team at Cal State Fullerton and strength training most days of the week. I'm also a huge follower of the NFL and Formula 1, with the Denver Broncos and McLaren Racing being my favorite teams/constructors. Lastly, I love to challenge myself. Every day I push myself to learn something new; whether it's enhancing my skills in Python, SQL, or writing blogs/personal stories, I'm focused on being 1% better every day.
